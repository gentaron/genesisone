About Mina

The mind
behind the vault.

Mina Eureka is a writer, strategist, and relentless thinker. She graduated from Lorentio International University's AI department — not to land a job, but to understand what was coming. She did.

Years of backpacking across three continents left her with notebooks full of questions and zero tolerance for corporate life. At 25, she launched Genesis Vault — a one-person media operation built on editorial precision, philosophical patience, and a hacker's sense of urgency.

Today she writes about AI systems, geopolitical fault lines, economic theory, and the philosophy nobody bothers to teach. She also freelances as an AI strategist for clients who prefer thinking over jargon.
